Our Story

More than coffee — it's a feeling.

Midnight Brew was born in the autumn of 2019 from a simple idea: what if your neighborhood had a place that felt like a warm embrace the moment you walked through the door? A spot where strangers become regulars, and regulars become friends.

We source single-origin beans from small family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasting them in-house every week to preserve their natural character. Every cup is a conversation between the farmer who grew it and the hands that brewed it.

Whether you're here for a quiet morning ritual, a late-night study session, or a slow weekend catch-up — pull up a chair. You're always welcome.
